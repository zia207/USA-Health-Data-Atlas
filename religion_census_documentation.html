U.S. County Religion Census (RCMS 2010) — Dataset Documentation

County-level religious congregations, adherents, and adherence rates from the Religious Congregations & Membership Study (RCMS) 2010 and the 2020 U.S. Religion Census (USRC Group Detail). The USA Religion Census explorer (usa_religion_census.html) maps counties for both years with up to 375 groups in 2020.

Metrics Congregations, adherents, adherence rate per 1,000 population

Definitions (RCMS)

Term Definition
Congregations Religious groups meeting regularly for worship
Adherents Members plus their children plus estimated regular non-member participants affiliated with a specific congregation (not self-identification)
Adherence rate Adherents per 1,000 residents (2010 county population)

RCMS adherent counts reflect religious affiliation with congregations, not survey self-identification. A person may identify with a faith tradition without being counted as an adherent if they do not participate in a counted congregation.

2020 USRC county schema

The 2020 layer is built from the 2020 Group by County sheet in 2020_USRC_Group_Detail.xlsx. Group codes are normalized to lowercase strings (zero-padded numerics, e.g. 081 for Catholic Church, 151 for LDS).

Column Type Description
fips string(5) County FIPS
county string County name
state_abbr string State abbreviation (derived from FIPS)
state string State name
population_2020 float County population estimated from adherent share of population
group_code string USRC group code
group_name string Denomination label
congregations float Congregation count
adherents float Adherent count
rate_per_1000 float Adherence rate per 1,000 population

County totals (tot) sum all reported denominations per county. Unlike 2010, the 2020 release does not include pre-aggregated tradition families (Evangelical, Mainline, etc.) in the Group Detail file.

Ten dominant groups

The explorer includes a Group catalog → 10 dominant groups view that aggregates county adherents and congregations into ten major U.S. religious traditions:

Code Group Share (approx.)
dom_evan Evangelical Protestants ~23% of adults
dom_cath Roman Catholics ~19–22% of adults
dom_mprt Mainline Protestants ~11% of adults
dom_bprt Historically Black Protestants ~5% of adults
dom_lds Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS) —
dom_judaism Judaism ~2% of adults
dom_islam Islam ~1% of adults
dom_hindu Hinduism ~1% of adults
dom_buddhist Buddhism ~1% of adults
dom_orthodox Orthodox Christians ~1% of adults

2010 (RCMS): uses tradition-family aggregates where available (evan, cath, mprt, bprt, lds, orth) and bundles Jewish, Muslim, Hindu, and Buddhist denominations from the codebook.

2020 (USRC): maps each of the 372 denomination codes to a dominant group using name-based rules and manual overrides (data/religion_census/dominant_groups.py). Unclassified denominations are excluded from these ten sums.

Coverage and limitations

  • Single-year snapshots: 2010 and 2020 are separate cross-sections (no time series).
  • Group codes differ by year: 2010 uses short RCMS codes (cath, lds); 2020 uses USRC numeric/alphanumeric codes (081, 151).
  • 2020 totals: tot is the sum of reported county denominations, not a separately reported USRC total row.
  • Undercounting: RCMS does not capture all religious participation (home churches, non-affiliated spirituality, groups that do not report).
  • Double counting: Tradition family totals (evan, mprt, etc.) are aggregates; do not sum families with tot or with individual denominations.
  • Missing values: Some groups report congregations only, or are absent in sparse counties; nulls are preserved.
  • 2020 refresh: Additional ARDA RCMSCY20 fields can supplement the USRC xlsx when available.

Example SQL (explorer)

Top counties by total adherents:

SELECT fips, county, state_abbr, val, unit
FROM religion_counties
WHERE group_code = 'tot' AND metric = 'adherents'
ORDER BY val DESC
LIMIT 25

Catholic adherence rate by state:

SELECT state_abbr, val, unit
FROM religion_states
WHERE group_code = 'cath' AND metric = 'rate'
ORDER BY val DESC

Counties with highest Evangelical Protestant adherents in Texas:

SELECT fips, county, state_abbr, val, unit
FROM religion_counties
WHERE state_abbr = 'TX' AND group_code = 'evan' AND metric = 'adherents'
ORDER BY val DESC
LIMIT 20
